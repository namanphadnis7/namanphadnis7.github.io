IceMania

IceMania is an ice-themed puzzle game where the player controls a cube. The objective is to reach the goal cube of every level while moving across a super slippery surface.


Download

Development

The game was developed in Unity 3D, using Javascript and C# scripts.

This was my first video game, which I made as a birthday surprise for my mom. I developed it in 3 days right before her b'day.

Final Game

Players control a green cube, and the objective is to reach the blue cube.


It’s not easy though! In your way are several obstacles and puzzles, and a LOT of ice.

The game mechanic was developed first before the theme. I initially just created the models in unity and added basic movement to them.


One of the movement features- kinetics, was funny to tamper with and I loved the mechanics of just moving the boxes around with absurd values. That’s when the idea of creating an ice-themed puzzle game struck me.
