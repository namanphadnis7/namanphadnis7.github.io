Dodge It!

Dodge It! is a projectile dodging high-score based game. The player controls a smiley face and has to dodge projectiles as long as possible.


Download

The game was developed in GameMaker Studio using GML scripts.

The longer the player dodges the obstacles, the higher the score goes. Get as high of a score as you can. How do you do that? Dodge it!
