Dodge it! 2

Dodge It! 2 is an endless runner set in the Naruto universe. Players need to dodge as many training dummies, bushes, and trees as they can to get as high a score as possible.


Download

Drawing Assets

Creating Sprites

I drew a basic stickman figure, and tried giving it a running animation. During the process, I realized I accidentally gave it a naruto-run, which fit the Naruto theme of the game perfectly.

Final Touches

After I had a clear picture of what I wanted to do, I gave my stick figure a little more detail, making it look like a young shinobi from the Naruto universe.

Making Game Objects

After making character sprites, I made the obstacles, and created game objects for every sprite. I then coded the functionality in GML and playtested it with myself and my friends.
