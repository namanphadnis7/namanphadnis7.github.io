Origin Story

I grew up in Bhopal, India - A small city famous for its pretty lakes, cute ducks in parks, and heavenly sandwiches. Gaming and designing were my favorite hobbies growing up.

I didn't care if I was editing videos, doodling, or making video games, as long as I could show something cool I made to my friends.


Growing up, this love for creative projects blossomed into creating better user experiences and designing simple products. For me, overcoming user problems through design gives me the same joy as I got in high school making games for my friends and watching their reactions playing them.


I want to continue creating amazing user experiences regardless of the medium.
