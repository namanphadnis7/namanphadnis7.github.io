About Me

I grew up in Bhopal, India - A small city famous for its pretty lakes, cute ducks in parks, and tasty sandwiches. Video games and content creation were my two favorite hobbies growing up. When I wasn't playing, I was designing games, editing videos, or making memes.


Growing up, this love for creative projects blossomed into creating better user experiences and designing products. For me, overcoming user problems through design gives me the same joy as I got in high school making a game for my friends and watching their reaction playing it.


I want to continue creating amazing user experiences regardless of the medium.
